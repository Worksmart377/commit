About the Commit Planner

The Handy Dandy Project Planner
Sometimes you just need a little help getting organized and keeping all of your research in one place.

This App is meant to assist Developers in project planning. You can add multiple projects, you can add multiple tasks to a project, you can add multiple journal entries to a task, and you can also search youtube for related tutorials to assist you with your production research.

  • Store your wonderful project ideas here!
  • Plan out your tasks for each project!
  • Give your self and due date and track your progress as you complete your tasks until your vision comes to fruition!
